PEI IV :

PEI V :
Floor coverings subject to intensified, continuous pedestrian traffic, with certain amounts of dirt stains in extreme conditions – for example, public places such as shopping centers, airports, hotel lobbies, public walkways, and industrial applications.

UNGLAZED or Full-Body Porcelain:
100% of the materials are porcelain. pressed and heated in the kiln. 0% glaze or printed coat
  • Single Charge: Top layer and base layer are pressed under high pressure and heated in high temperatures. Top layer is the design layer and it has a thickness around 2mm
  • Double Charge: The top layer is a 4mm thick porcelain layer, pressed and heated to the base layer. The thick top or design layer makes it super durable against abrasions
  • Homogenous Full-Body: consistent composition of the porcelain material throughout the entire body of the tile, front, side of the tile should be the same color and texture
GLAZED Porcelain :
less than 1mm layer or coat is digitally printed on the surface of porcelain body
